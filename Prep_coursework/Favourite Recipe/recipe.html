Today's Favourite Reciple is...Trinidadian Doubles

THE ULTIMATE STREET FOOD Doubles is THE ULTIMATE Trini street food. It doesn’t get any better than this: it’s relatively cheap, tasty, wholesome, filling, fresh, piping hot and fits nicely in the palm of your hand.

DOUBLES RECIPE INGREDIENTS

bara

  • 1 lb flour
  • 1 tsp saffron powder (tumeric)
  • 1 tbsp. baking powder
  • tsp yeast
  • 1/4 tsp. sugar
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tbsp. oil
  • oil for frying

DOUBLES RECIPE INGREDIENTS

bara

  • 2 cups channa, soaked overnight
  • 10 cups water, for boiling
  • 2 tsp. baking soda
  • 2 tbsp. oil
  • 1 tsp. curry
  • 1 tsp. saffron (tumeric)
  • 1 tsp. geera (cumin)
  • 1 tsp. masala
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped finely
  • 1/2 onion, chopped finely
  • 5 leaves chadon beni ( bandhania), chopped finely
  • salt and pepper to taste

DIRECTIONS

Mix all the ingredients dry in a bowl.

Add the water and knead until a smooth dough is formed.

Then add the tablespoon of oil over the dough.

Cover with a cloth and leave to rest for 1 hour or until double its size.

The dough looks ready now. Spread a thin layer oil over the surface you’re using and make little balls about 2 inches in diameter.

Heat the oil over a medium to high flame… Fry on both sides for 7 to 10 seconds
